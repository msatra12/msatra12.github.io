Who I am

I'm an IT Specialist with 4+ years of hands-on experience supporting enterprise infrastructure across a multi-campus university. I moved from IT Service Desk to IT Specialist in under two years — taking ownership of endpoint management, security compliance, and M365 administration along the way.

I'm currently pursuing roles in IT support, systems administration, and infrastructure where I can apply hands-on enterprise experience and keep growing.
